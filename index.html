Urchade Zaratiana

I am a PhD student at Laboratoire Informatique de Paris Nord (LIPN), under the supervision of Nadi Tomeh and Thierry Charnois. My research focuses on structured prediction for Natural Language Processing.

I am interested in the science of deep learning in general, including topics such as domain generalization, zero/few-shot learning, instruction tuning, learning under noisy data/labels, scaling laws, and intriguing phenomenons such as the 'double descent', the 'grokking mechanism' and the 'simplicity bias'.

During my PhD, I have worked on the following research problems:

  • Zero-shot Learning for Information Extraction (2023-)
    • GLiNER (Zaratiana et al., 2024): A model designed for Zero-shot Named Entity Recognition (GitHub).
    • GraphER (Zaratiana et al., 2024): An end-to-end model for zero-shot joint entity and relation extraction (GitHub).
  • Language model pretraining (2024-)
    Training a BERT-like model without positional encoding (Zaratiana et al., ICLR 2024 Tiny Paper) (Link)
  • Constrained decoding of language model (2022-)
    Autoregressive text to graph model for Information extraction: preliminary version published at ICML 2023 workshop (Zaratiana et al., 2023) (Link), and final version at AAAI 2024 (Zaratiana et al., 2024) (Link)
  • Detection of machine-generated text (2024-)
    Our system ranked top 2 at SemEval "Multidomain, Multimodel and Multilingual Machine-Generated Text Detection" shared task using 300M parameters model. Top 1 used a 70b model. (Zaratiana et al., 2024) (Link)
  • Efficient and exact decoding for information extraction (2021-)
    • Named Entity Recognition as Structured Span Prediction (Zaratiana et al., 2022) (Link)
    • EnriCO: Constrained decoding of information extraction using logical rules. (Zaratiana et al., 2024) (Link)
  • Graph (structure) learning (2021-)
    • GraphER: End-to-end graph structure learning of joint entity and relation extraction (Zaratiana et al., 2024) (Link)
    • GNNer: using graph (and Graph Neural Network) to implicitly constrain the output of neural network (Zaratiana et al., 2022) (Link)
  • Improved training algorithm (loss functions) (2022-)
    • Filtered Semi-CRF (Zaratiana et al., 2023) (Link)
    • Global Span Selection (Zaratiana et al., 2022) (Link)
  • Arabic Information extraction (2023-)
    • Cross-Dialectal Named Entity Recognition in Arabic (El Khbir et al., 2023) (Link)
    • A Span-Based Approach for Flat and Nested Arabic Named Entity Recognition: Top 1 system at Wojood NER shared task (El Khbir et al., 2023) (Link)
